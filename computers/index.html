Legacy & Retro Hardware

FluxLinux is exceptionally lightweight, making it the perfect choice for breathing new life into older hardware. By focusing on minimal resource overhead and efficient process management, even decade-old laptops feel snappy and responsive again.

If you have an old machine lying around, FluxLinux is the retro-friendly way to make it relevant in the modern era — without sacrificing control or security.
